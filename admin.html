Our Services

Complete Blood Count

A comprehensive blood test, or CBC, assesses red and white blood cells, platelets, hemoglobin, and hematocrit, aiding in the diagnosis of conditions like anemia, infection, and clotting disorders.

Read More

Kidney Function Test

Kidney function tests evaluate the efficiency of the kidneys in filtering waste & balancing electrolytes. They include measurements of creatinine, and urea, aiding in detecting kidney dysfunction.

Read More

Liver Function Test

Liver function tests assess the health and performance of the liver by measuring enzymes, proteins, and other substances in the blood. They help diagnose liver diseases and monitor treatment effectiveness.

Read More

Lipid Profile

Lipid profile assesses levels of cholesterol and fats in the blood, including HDL, LDL, and triglycerides. It helps evaluate cardiovascular risk and guides preventive measures.

Read More

C-Reactive Protine

C-reactive protein (CRP) is a marker of inflammation in the body. Elevated levels indicate inflammation, aiding in diagnosing and monitoring infections and cardiovascular diseases.

Read More

Blood Sugar

Blood sugar refers to glucose levels in the bloodstream, crucial for energy. Monitoring it helps manage conditions like diabetes, preventing complications.
